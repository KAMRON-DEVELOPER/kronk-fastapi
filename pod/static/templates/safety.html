Safety Standards

Last updated: July 22, 2025

Kronk is committed to providing a safe experience for all users. While our app is not specifically directed toward children under 13, we take steps to follow child safety guidelines where applicable.

Content and User Interaction

  • We do not allow sexually explicit, violent, or abusive content.
  • User-generated content is moderated, and users can report inappropriate material.
  • There is no public chat or anonymous messaging available to minors.

Privacy and Data Collection

  • We do not collect sensitive data from children.
  • No targeted advertising is shown based on user profiling.
  • User accounts must be 13 years or older to register.

Parental Guidance

We recommend parental guidance for users under 18. Kronk is intended for general audiences but may contain features (e.g., social feeds) that require discretion for younger users.
