Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 19, 2025

These guidelines are here to protect you and others. They outline the acceptable use of Kronk and what behavior we prohibit to keep the platform safe, inclusive, and enjoyable.

🔒 Zero Tolerance Policy

  • ❌ No hate speech, racism, or harassment
  • ❌ No threats, doxxing, or inciting violence
  • ❌ No sexually explicit, illegal, or violent content
  • ⚠ Violations may result in immediate bans or legal reporting

✅ Respectful Behavior

  • ✅ Treat others with kindness and empathy
  • ✅ Share constructive feedback
  • ✅ Use block and report tools when needed

📸 Content Rules

  • ✅ Share only content you own or have rights to
  • ❌ No spamming, mass-advertising, or bot promotion
  • ❌ Don’t impersonate individuals, brands, or organizations
  • ❌ Avoid NSFW, disturbing, or misleading content

🔧 Moderation

We manually review all reports. Serious offenses will result in permanent account bans without warning. Minor infractions may result in temporary restrictions or education.

🚨 Violation Levels

  • 1st Warning: Content removed and warning issued
  • 2nd Warning: Temporary suspension
  • 3rd Strike: Permanent ban and possible IP restriction
